### Problem Context

Infrastructure environments rely on strict electrical isolation procedures to protect personnel and prevent severe incidents.

However, operational complexity, environmental variability, and procedural gaps can introduce elevated risk.

This dashboard explores how isolation failures and severe incidents are distributed across different infrastructure environments.

---

### What This Dashboard Does

* Classifies infrastructure environments
* Maps severe incident locations
* Highlights isolation risk indicators
* Tracks trends across operational conditions

The goal is to provide a simplified, high-signal view of where operational risk may be concentrated.

---

### Example Insight Framework

**Signal**
Certain infrastructure environments show higher clustering of severe incidents.

**Implication**
Operational complexity or procedural inconsistencies may increase isolation risk in these areas.

**Next Step**
Operations teams may evaluate lockout/tagout procedures, training coverage, or inspection frequency in high-risk environments.
